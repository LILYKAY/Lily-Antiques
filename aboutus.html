About Lily Antiques

Established in 2015 in Uganda, Lily Antiques Uganda LTD specializes in tribal African art and antiquities.
We are specialists in the traditional arts of East and Central Africa. We do business over the internet,
and a private gallery (by appointment) that is if you happen to be in Kampala.
Inspiration
My name is Kamunya Lilian Wanjiku, I have been a dealer of tribal African art and antiquities for 05 years now.
I specialize in Congo, Tanzania, Uganda, Kenya and South Sudan but also minimum artifacts from Rwanda and Burundi.
I found myself growing an interest in this trade as a result;
I developed a passion for tribal art after curating in a contemporary art gallery
TULIFANYA GALLERY for numerous years after a while I decided to specialize in old
field collected pieces, for those curious, collecting or dealers and are all welcome by
APPOINTMENT on our humble gallery on GAYAZA ROAD 8 miles from city centre of kampala,
Over the years of my field trips I have accumulated quite a collection,
I am still thrilled by my extensive network of a local field collectors in the region.
